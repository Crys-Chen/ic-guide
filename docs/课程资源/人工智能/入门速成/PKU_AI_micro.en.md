# Peking University: AI Mini-Course for High-School Students (10 Lectures)

## Descriptions

- Offered by: Peking University
- Prerequisites: None
- Programming Languages: None
- Instructors: PKU Institute for AI team
- Difficulty: 🌟
- Estimated Hours: ~10 hours

10 AI literacy micro-lectures officially released by PKU on Bilibili, focused on six areas: **computer vision, NLP, cognitive reasoning, machine learning, intelligent robotics, multi-agent**. Uses plain language to convey the full AI panorama — the lowest-barrier intro.

Well-suited for non-technical backgrounds or absolute beginners as a first step into AI.
