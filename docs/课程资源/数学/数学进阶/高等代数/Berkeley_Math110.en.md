# UC Berkeley Math 110: Linear Algebra

## Descriptions

- Offered by: UC Berkeley
- Prerequisites: Math 53/54 (multivariable calc + intro linear algebra)
- Programming Languages: None
- Difficulty: 🌟🌟🌟🌟
- Estimated Hours: ~40 hours per semester

Berkeley's core proof-based undergraduate linear algebra course. Covers vector spaces, inner product spaces, dual spaces, Jordan normal form, and quadratic forms — fully matching the content of Chinese "advanced algebra" both semesters. Many instructor's notes are public; Prof. Paulin's are particularly well-regarded.

Largely interchangeable with MIT 18.700; Math 110 leans slightly more toward geometry and multilinear algebra, with deeper treatment of quadratic forms and orthogonal groups.
