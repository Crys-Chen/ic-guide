# USTC · Ling Qing: Optimization Theory (Convex Optimization)

## Descriptions

- Offered by: USTC (Prof. Ling Qing, now at Sun Yat-sen University)
- Prerequisites: Calculus, linear algebra, probability
- Programming Languages: MATLAB / Python (CVXPY)
- Instructor: Prof. Ling Qing
- Difficulty: 🌟🌟🌟🌟
- Estimated Hours: ~60 hours

The de-facto Chinese-language convex optimization course on Bilibili. Taught by Prof. Ling Qing, with content fully aligned with Stephen Boyd's textbook *Convex Optimization* — every proof is stepped through in Chinese. The best Chinese companion to Stanford EE364A.

Videos have been remastered in 4K; an accompanying CSDN study guide of ~55 notes is available.
