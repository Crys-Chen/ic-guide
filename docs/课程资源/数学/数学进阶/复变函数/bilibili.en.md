# Bilibili: Complex functions and integral transforms.

## Descriptions

- Offered by: None
- Prerequisites: Calculus
- Programming Languages: None
- Difficulty: 🌟🌟🌟

For engineering students, the application of complex functions and integral transforms is not very extensive and is mainly used in signal processing. Therefore, in-depth study is not necessary. You can easily self-study using Professor Li Hong's textbook from Huazhong University of Science and Technology, and search online for explanations if needed.
