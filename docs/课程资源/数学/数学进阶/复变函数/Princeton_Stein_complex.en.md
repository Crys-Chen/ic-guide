# Princeton · Stein Complex Analysis (Video Companion)

## Descriptions

- Offered by: Princeton (based on Stein-Shakarchi textbook)
- Prerequisites: Real analysis, intro topology
- Programming Languages: None
- Difficulty: 🌟🌟🌟🌟🌟
- Estimated Hours: Full YouTube playlist

A YouTube companion (community / student-recorded explanations) to Volume II of Princeton's four-volume Stein-Shakarchi analysis series, *Complex Analysis*. Goes from holomorphic functions all the way to the ζ-function and the Prime Number Theorem — a graduate-level course, with depth far beyond undergraduate complex analysis.

Suitable for upper-level math undergraduates, math grad students, or engineering students drawn to the Stein series.
