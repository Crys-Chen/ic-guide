# Zhejiang University: Mastering Digital System Design (National Quality Course)

## Descriptions

- Offered by: Zhejiang University
- Prerequisites: Digital logic basics, intro Verilog
- Programming Languages: Verilog
- Difficulty: 🌟🌟🌟
- Estimated Hours: One-semester MOOC (theory + lab integrated)

ZJU's national-class quality MOOC that fuses "Digital System Design" and its lab course into a single offering, benchmarked against top international engineering courses. Theory and Verilog practice are tightly coupled — combinational and sequential logic all the way to processor-core design.

A more systematic Chinese-language alternative to HDLBits; better suited for *learning* digital system design rather than just practicing Verilog syntax.
