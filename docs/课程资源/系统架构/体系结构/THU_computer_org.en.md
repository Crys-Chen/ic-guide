# Tsinghua · Wang Cheng / Liu Weidong: Computer Organization (National Quality Course)

## Descriptions

- Offered by: Tsinghua University
- Prerequisites: Digital logic
- Programming Languages: Assembly, Verilog
- Instructors: Wang Cheng, Liu Weidong
- Difficulty: 🌟🌟🌟
- Estimated Hours: ~50 lectures / 40 hours

A national-class quality course taught by Profs. Wang Cheng and Liu Weidong, paired with the **TEC-2000 teaching machine final project** — the legendary "build a computer in three weeks". The most classic Chinese-language computer organization course, covering data representation, ALU, control unit, memory, and I/O.

A good Chinese hardware-perspective foundation before CSAPP.
