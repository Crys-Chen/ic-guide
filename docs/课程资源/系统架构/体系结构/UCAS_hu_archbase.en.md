# UCAS · Hu Weiwu: Computer Architecture (Loongson)

## Descriptions

- Offered by: University of Chinese Academy of Sciences
- Prerequisites: Computer organization
- Programming Languages: LoongArch assembly, Verilog
- Instructor: Prof. Hu Weiwu (Loongson chief architect)
- Difficulty: 🌟🌟🌟🌟
- Estimated Hours: ~60 hours

Taught by Hu Weiwu, chief architect of Loongson — the course weaves China's domestic CPU R&D experience into the teaching of architecture. The companion textbook *Computer Architecture Fundamentals* (3rd ed.) is free as a GitBook.

The representative "industry-first-line perspective" Chinese-language architecture course; complements academic-style courses.
