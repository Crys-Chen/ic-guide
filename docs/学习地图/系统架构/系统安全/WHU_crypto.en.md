# Wuhan University: Cryptography (National Quality Course)

## Descriptions

- Offered by: Wuhan University (National Cybersecurity School)
- Prerequisites: Discrete math, basic number theory
- Programming Languages: None
- Instructors: WHU Cybersecurity School team
- Difficulty: 🌟🌟🌟🌟
- Estimated Hours: ~40 hours

A national-class quality course from Wuhan University's National School of Cybersecurity, systematically teaching cryptographic foundations and modern protocols. The most authoritative Chinese-language open course on information security / cryptography; pairs well with CS161/CS366-style English security courses.
