# National University of Defense Technology: Software Engineering (National Quality Course)

## Descriptions

- Offered by: National University of Defense Technology
- Prerequisites: Intro programming
- Programming Languages: None
- Instructors: Qi Zhichang, Mao Xinjun, et al.
- Difficulty: 🌟🌟🌟
- Estimated Hours: 50 hours

NUDT's flagship software engineering course — a national-class quality open course. Prof. Mao Xinjun also chairs the Ministry of Education's "101 Plan" virtual teaching unit for software engineering. Systematically covers software processes, requirements engineering, design, testing, and maintenance.
