# Zhejiang University · Wu Fei: Artificial Intelligence — Models and Algorithms (National Quality Course)

## Descriptions

- Offered by: Zhejiang University
- Prerequisites: High-school math
- Programming Languages: None
- Instructor: Prof. Wu Fei
- Difficulty: 🌟🌟
- Estimated Hours: ~30 hours

A first-batch national top-class online course. Systematically lays out the AI framework along three lineages: **symbolic, connectionist, behaviorist**. Paired with the Ministry of Education's "101 Plan" textbook *Introduction to Artificial Intelligence* — well-suited for zero-baseline learners building a full AI panorama.
