# Xi'an Jiaotong University: Complex Variables (National Quality Course)

## Descriptions

- Offered by: Xi'an Jiaotong University
- Prerequisites: Calculus
- Programming Languages: None
- Instructors: XJTU national-class math teaching team
- Difficulty: 🌟🌟🌟
- Estimated Hours: 32 lectures (main version) / 68 lectures (extended)

XJTU's national-class quality course, built by their national teaching team and using Wang Miansen's *Complex Variables* (Higher Education Press) — a "11th Five-Year Plan" national-level textbook. The most reputable formal Chinese-language complex-analysis course on Bilibili, covering complex numbers, complex functions, complex integration, residue theorem, and conformal mapping.
