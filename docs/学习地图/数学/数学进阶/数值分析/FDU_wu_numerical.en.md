# Fudan University · Wu Zongmin: Numerical Approximation

## Descriptions

- Offered by: Fudan University, Dept. of Mathematics
- Prerequisites: Mathematical analysis, linear algebra
- Programming Languages: MATLAB (optional)
- Instructor: Prof. Wu Zongmin
- Difficulty: 🌟🌟🌟🌟
- Estimated Hours: 42 lectures

Prof. Wu Zongmin's course at Fudan's Department of Mathematics, the representative Chinese course on function approximation. The deepest Chinese-language treatment of the **interpolation / approximation / least-squares** portion of numerical analysis. Compared with a general "numerical methods" course, focuses more on the theoretical foundations of approximation theory (Bernstein polynomials, Chebyshev nodes, splines, etc.).

A "theoretical depth complement" to MIT 18.330: MIT 18.330 leans algorithmic and engineering; Wu Zongmin's leans mathematical principles.
