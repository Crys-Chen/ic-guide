# BUPT: Modern Cryptography (Chinese National Quality Course)

## Descriptions

- Offered by: Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications
- Prerequisites: Abstract algebra, basic number theory
- Programming Languages: None (theory course)
- Difficulty: 🌟🌟🌟
- Estimated Hours: ~48 hours

A national-class quality online course — the most systematic Chinese-language coverage of modern cryptography on Bilibili. Covers stream ciphers, block ciphers (DES/AES), public-key cryptography (RSA/ElGamal/ECC), digital signatures, hash functions, and full cryptographic protocols. Companion textbook is Yang Bo's *Modern Cryptography* (Tsinghua Press) — the de facto cryptography textbook in mainland China.

Complements Stanford Boneh's course as the "Chinese systematic" counterpart to the English authoritative version.
