# Peking University: Discrete Mathematics (National Quality Course)

## Descriptions

- Offered by: Peking University
- Prerequisites: High-school math
- Programming Languages: None
- Instructors: Qu Wanling, Geng Suyun, Wang Hanbin, Liu Tian
- Difficulty: 🌟🌟🌟
- Estimated Hours: 194 lectures / ~144 hours

The national quality course chaired by Prof. Qu Wanling at PKU. Complete on Bilibili, covering all **six pillars**: mathematical logic, set theory, algebraic structures, combinatorics, graph theory, and elementary number theory. Uses the classic PKU "black-cover" textbook *Discrete Mathematics*.

The most authoritative Chinese-language open course in discrete math. Complements UCB CS70 / CS126: the PKU version emphasizes mathematical rigor, CS70 emphasizes CS applications — best learned together.
