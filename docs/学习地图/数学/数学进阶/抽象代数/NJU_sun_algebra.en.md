# Nanjing University · Sun Zhi-Wei: Modern Algebra

## Descriptions

- Offered by: Nanjing University
- Prerequisites: Advanced algebra (linear algebra + polynomial theory)
- Programming Languages: None
- Instructor: Prof. Sun Zhi-Wei
- Difficulty: 🌟🌟🌟🌟
- Estimated Hours: 104 lectures / ~60–80 hours

A national-class top undergraduate course (2025) reflecting Prof. Sun Zhi-Wei's 20+ years of teaching abstract algebra at NJU. Systematically covers groups, rings, and fields, using his own textbook *Modern Algebra* (NJU Press), all the way to Galois theory.

The style follows the traditional rigorous Chinese math-department format — definition, theorem, proof, example. The most systematic Chinese-language open course in abstract algebra; pairs well with MIT 18.701/702 (Artin) as a Chinese counterpart.
