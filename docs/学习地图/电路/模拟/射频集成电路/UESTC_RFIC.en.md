# UESTC: RF Integrated Circuits

## Descriptions

- Offered by: University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC)
- Prerequisites: Analog IC design
- Programming Languages: None (with Cadence labs)
- Instructors: Prof. Fan Yong's team
- Difficulty: 🌟🌟🌟🌟🌟
- Estimated Hours: 9 lectures

UESTC's RFIC MOOC built by Prof. Fan Yong's team. Structurally mirrors Razavi's RFIC: CMOS process/devices → single-stage amplifiers → noise → transceivers → LNA → mixers → passive devices → PA. A flagship Chinese RFIC course from one of China's top RF/MMW groups; complements Fudan's Yin Yun version.
