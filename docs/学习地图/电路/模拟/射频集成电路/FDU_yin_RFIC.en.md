# Fudan University · Yin Yun: RF Circuit Design

## Descriptions

- Offered by: Fudan University
- Prerequisites: Analog ICs, high-frequency electronics
- Programming Languages: None (with Cadence simulation)
- Instructor: Prof. Yin Yun
- Difficulty: 🌟🌟🌟🌟🌟
- Estimated Hours: One graduate semester

Taught by Prof. Yin Yun (6 ISSCC papers, 9 JSSC papers) at Fudan's School of Microelectronics. The highest-rated Chinese RFIC course on Bilibili, directly mirroring Razavi RF / UCB EE142 — covering noise, transceivers, and PAs for a Chinese-speaking audience. The most authoritative Chinese-language RFIC video course.

Suitable for EE / microelectronics grad students or upper-level undergrads who have already taken analog IC design.
