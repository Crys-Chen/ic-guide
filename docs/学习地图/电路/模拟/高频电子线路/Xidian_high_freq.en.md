# Xidian University: High-Frequency Electronic Circuits (National Quality Course)

## Descriptions

- Offered by: Xidian University
- Prerequisites: Circuit analysis, analog electronics
- Programming Languages: None
- Instructors: Built by Zeng Xingwen / Chen Jian team; MOOC by Deng Jun, Zhao Jianxun
- Difficulty: 🌟🌟🌟🌟
- Estimated Hours: ~64 hours

The most authoritative Chinese-language course on high-frequency electronic circuits, built by Xidian's national teaching team. Companion textbook is Zeng Xingwen's "11th Five-Year Plan" national textbook from HEP. MOOC version praised on Zhihu for being "comfortable and clear". Covers small-signal frequency-selective amplifiers, AM/FM, mixers, oscillators, and PLLs.

The Chinese counterpart to EE613 / MIT 6.776 for high-frequency analog.
