# Sichuan University · Liu Changjun: RF Communication Circuits

## Descriptions

- Offered by: Sichuan University
- Prerequisites: High-frequency electronics or equivalent
- Programming Languages: None (with CAD-based design labs)
- Instructor: Prof. Liu Changjun
- Difficulty: 🌟🌟🌟🌟
- Estimated Hours: One semester

Taught by Prof. Liu Changjun at SCU, one of Bilibili's top-15 RF courses. Sits **between** "high-frequency electronics" and full RFIC — from transmission-line lumped models and CAD design platforms to RF amplifier / PA design, pushing discrete-component high-frequency design toward engineering practice.

Good transitional course between Xidian's high-frequency course and full RFIC.
