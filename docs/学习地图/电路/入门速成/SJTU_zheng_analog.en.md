# Shanghai Jiao Tong University · Zheng Yihui: Fundamentals of Analog Electronics

## Descriptions

- Offered by: Shanghai Jiao Tong University
- Prerequisites: Circuit principles
- Programming Languages: None
- Instructor: Prof. Zheng Yihui
- Difficulty: 🌟🌟🌟
- Estimated Hours: 44 lectures

Bilibili's top-rated Chinese-language analog electronics course, widely considered slightly stronger than the Tsinghua / Hua Chengying version. Prof. Zheng's treatment of small-signal equivalent models is particularly clear, and the semiconductor-device physics intuition is excellent — the best Chinese-language companion for analog electronics intro.

Companion textbook is Tong Shibai / Hua Chengying, *Fundamentals of Analog Electronics* (Higher Education Press); complements the Tsinghua version well.
