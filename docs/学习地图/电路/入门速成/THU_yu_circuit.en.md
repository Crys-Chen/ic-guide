# Tsinghua · Yu Xinjie: Circuit Principles (National Quality Course)

## Descriptions

- Offered by: Tsinghua University
- Prerequisites: Calculus, linear algebra, college physics (E&M)
- Programming Languages: None
- Instructor: Prof. Yu Xinjie
- Difficulty: 🌟🌟🌟
- Estimated Hours: ~48–64 hours

Tsinghua's first MOOC, a national-class quality course taught by Prof. Yu Xinjie. The hallmark is **Week 0**, which fills in prerequisite calculus, linear algebra, and physics — beginner-friendly. Systematically covers resistive circuits, dynamic circuits, sinusoidal steady-state, three-phase circuits, and two-port networks.

The most authoritative Chinese-language intro circuit course; the Chinese counterpart to UCB EE16A&B.
